IT & Networking

For me, IT is not just about staying behind a screen typing code. It is above all a desire to understand how the digital world works. Since I was little, I have always needed to take things apart to see what was inside.

This is also what drove me to create Skynium: I love that moment when I manage to fix a problem that was blocking someone. I love making technology simple and useful for others.

And for my personal pleasure, I have fun building networks, hosting my own servers, or tinkering with Linux. It's like an infinite construction set where there is always something new to learn.
