EUROVISION SCENOGRAPHY & TECH

For me, Eurovision is not just a TV show; it is really what inspired me to create the Skynium Arena. I love seeing how they use thousands of lights to transform the venue for every song, the impressive number of cameras flying everywhere, and above all, the energy of the crowd that you can feel even through the screen. It is this "wow" feeling and this mix of technique and spectacle that I want to succeed in recreating in my own projects.

If I had to choose my best year, it is clearly Malmö 2024. I found the visuals magnificent, especially the use of suspended LED cubes and the theme with colorful neons. It was ultra-modern and dynamic. Furthermore, it was the return of Petra Mede, my favorite actress and presenter. This is the third time she has hosted, and for me, she is the best: she has a unique humor and commands the stage like no one else.
